PU Wall Panels

Book-Match Panels

Mirror-veined sheets, cut in sequence.

Book-matched panels are sliced from the same block and hung in mirrored order, so the vein pattern flows continuously across a feature wall. Used for lobbies, reception counters and statement walls where symmetry reads as craft.

  • MaterialNatural stone veneer / engineered veneer
  • Panel sizeUp to 1.2 m × 2.8 m
  • SequencingNumbered & supplied in book order
UV Marble Sheets

Marble look, a fraction of the weight.

UV-coated marble-finish sheets give the depth and gloss of natural marble without the weight, porosity or install cost of stone. Suited to feature walls, wardrobe shutters, counters and lift lobbies where natural marble isn't practical.

  • Thickness3 to 6mm sheet options
  • FinishHigh-gloss UV coat, scratch resistant
  • Sheet sizeUp to 1.22 m × 2.44 m
  • ApplicationWalls, counters, shutters, ceilings
3D Panels

Sculptural texture for accent walls.

Textured 3D panels in geometric and organic reliefs, used to break up flat drywall on accent walls, reception counters and feature ceilings. Available primed for paint or in factory finishes.

  • MaterialGypsum / PU / MDF core
  • Panel size500 mm × 500 mm modular tiles
  • FinishPaint-ready or factory colour
