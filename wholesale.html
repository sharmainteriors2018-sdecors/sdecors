Module 01 · Eligibility

Who qualifies for trade pricing.

SDecors runs one catalogue at two sets of terms. Retail buyers use the standard quote form; the businesses below apply here for trade pricing, credit terms and dedicated support.

Retail Showrooms & Dealers

Businesses selling blinds, wallpaper, panels or flooring to end customers from a physical or online storefront.

Contractors, Builders & Architects

Firms specifying or executing fit-outs who order directly for client sites, rather than reselling from stock.

Distributors & Regional Stockists

Businesses carrying inventory to supply smaller dealers or contractors within a city or region.

Module 02 · Minimum Order Quantities

MOQs are set per category, after your BOQ.

Fabric, substrate and finish all affect minimums, so we confirm an exact MOQ once we see your bill of quantities rather than quoting a single blanket figure. As a general shape:

Window Blinds

Minimums vary by fabric and mechanism. Sample sets are available for spec approval before an order is placed.

Wallpaper & Wall Panels

Ordered by roll or sheet batch; book-match and UV marble runs carry higher minimums due to sequencing and slab sizing.

Wooden & Carpet Flooring

Set by total area rather than piece count, with lower minimums on stocked ranges than on custom finishes.

Multi-Category Projects

Combined orders across categories are common on full fit-outs and are quoted as one consolidated MOQ.

New dealers with no order history are welcome to start with a smaller trial order to test quality and lead times before scaling to full trade volumes.

Module 03 · Slab-Based Pricing

Pricing moves in slabs as volume grows.

Rather than one fixed trade discount, pricing steps down in slabs tied to registration and order volume. Your exact slab and rate are confirmed at onboarding and reviewed as your order history builds.

Slab 1 · Retail

Standard listed pricing. No registration required — used for single-room and one-off orders.

Slab 2 · Registered Dealer

Unlocked on GST-verified onboarding. Preferential per-unit rates on standard catalogue items.

Slab 3 · Volume Partner

For dealers with a consistent order history or a standing annual volume commitment. Lower per-unit rates across more categories.

Slab 4 · Distributor

Regional stockists carrying inventory across multiple dealers. Our most preferential pricing tier, evaluated case by case.

Module 04 · Credit Terms

Terms build with order history.

Every trade account starts on advance payment. Credit is extended as an order history is established, not offered upfront.

  • New dealersAdvance payment on first orders
  • Established dealersPartial advance, balance on dispatch
  • Volume partnersBilling-cycle credit, reviewed periodically
  • InvoicingGST invoice with every trade shipment
  • ApprovalConfirmed by our accounts team at onboarding

Module 06 · Display-Board Support

Sell from samples, not from stock.

Onboarded dealers can request physical display material for their showroom, so customers can see and feel the range without you carrying deep inventory.

Category Sample Kits

Blind fabric fans, wallpaper sample books, and panel, marble or flooring swatch chips for each category you carry.

Branded Showroom Signage

FAQ

Common questions from prospective dealers.

Who can apply to become an SDecors dealer?

Registered retail showrooms, contractors and builders, architects and interior designers, and regional distributors or stockists. Homeowners and one-off buyers should use the standard quote form instead.

Is there a registration fee to become a dealer?

No. There is no fee to apply. Onboarding is based on business documentation and, where relevant, an opening trade order.

Can I start with a trial order before committing to full dealer volumes?

Yes. Most new dealers begin with a smaller trial order to confirm quality, lead times and fit before scaling up to their full trade volume.

Do you work with distributors outside Delhi NCR?

Yes. SDecors dispatches pan-India, and regional distributor and stockist arrangements are evaluated per city and category.

Installation Scope, Explained

Dealers often ask what we can do for their end customer beyond supplying material. Here's exactly what's covered.

Active Installation Hubs

Full field installation by our in-house team currently runs across Delhi NCR (Delhi, Gurugram, Noida, Faridabad, Ghaziabad).

Outside NCR, we dispatch material with detailed fitting instructions for your team or contractor. On-site SDecors installation elsewhere is evaluated case by case for larger trade volumes — flag it in your application if it matters for your territory.

Measurement Visit Charges

Within Delhi NCR, an on-site measurement visit is complimentary once an order is confirmed and scheduled.

A standalone measurement visit, requested before any order is confirmed, carries a nominal visit charge, credited back against the order if it proceeds. Outside NCR, we work from dimensions, photos or drawings shared with us.

Dismantling & Scaffolding

Removal of existing blinds, wallpaper or flooring is available as an add-on, quoted after our team sees the site or photos.

Scaffolding or high-access equipment for tall walls, double-height spaces or facades is arranged on request for an additional charge. If scaffolding is already on site, we coordinate around it at no extra cost.
