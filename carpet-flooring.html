FAQ

Common questions from buyers.

What's the difference between broadloom and tile carpet?

Broadloom is supplied in large rolls and glued down across a full floor, giving a seamless look for big open areas, while modular tiles are laid individually, which makes partial repairs, cable access and reconfiguration much easier over time.

Is fire-rated carpet available?

Yes, fire-rated carpet is available on request for commercial and hospitality projects with specific compliance requirements; let us know the rating your project needs.

How is carpet flooring installed?

Carpet is installed either glue-down, bonding the material directly to the subfloor for a permanent fit, or tile-and-release, using a low-tack adhesive so individual tiles can be lifted and replaced without disturbing the rest of the floor.

Carpeting an office or auditorium?

Send us the area and traffic type, and we'll recommend a pile and format and send pricing.
