FAQ

Common questions from buyers.

What's the difference between engineered and laminate wood flooring?

Engineered flooring has a real wood veneer layer over a stable core, giving it an authentic look and feel with better longevity, while laminate uses a printed wear layer over a high-density fibreboard core at a lower cost.

Is underlay included with wooden flooring orders?

Yes, matching underlay is available on request with every wooden flooring order, along with fitting guidance for your installation team.

What warranty comes with wooden flooring?

Warranty terms depend on the specific product line, with engineered ranges typically carrying warranties of up to 15 years; ask us for the terms on your chosen range.
