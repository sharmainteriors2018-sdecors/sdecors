Installation Scope, Explained

Not every order includes on-site installation. Here's exactly what's covered before you request a quote.

Active Installation Hubs

Full field installation by our in-house team currently runs across Delhi NCR (Delhi, Gurugram, Noida, Faridabad, Ghaziabad).

Outside NCR, we dispatch material with detailed fitting instructions for your site contractor. On-site SDecors installation elsewhere is evaluated case by case for larger trade orders — ask us before you order if this matters to your project.

Measurement Visit Charges

Within Delhi NCR, an on-site measurement visit is complimentary once your enquiry is confirmed and scheduled.

A standalone measurement visit, requested before any order is confirmed, carries a nominal visit charge, credited back against your order if you go ahead. Outside NCR, we work from dimensions, photos or drawings you share with us.

Dismantling & Scaffolding

Removal of existing blinds, wallpaper or flooring is available as an add-on, quoted after our team sees the site or your photos.

Scaffolding or high-access equipment for tall walls, double-height spaces or facades is arranged on request for an additional charge. If scaffolding is already on site, we coordinate around it at no extra cost.
