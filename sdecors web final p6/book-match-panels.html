FAQ

Common questions from buyers.

What is a book-match panel?

Book-match panels are sliced from the same block of stone or veneer and hung in mirrored order, so the vein pattern flows continuously across adjoining panels, like the pages of an open book.

What's the difference between book-match panels and UV marble sheets?

Book-match panels are cut from a single block (natural stone or engineered veneer) and sequenced for a mirrored, continuous vein. UV marble sheets are a lighter, UV-coated marble-look surface material and don't carry sequenced veining across panels.

What panel sizes and materials are available?

Panels are available in natural stone veneer or engineered veneer, up to 1.2m x 2.8m, numbered and supplied in book order so installers can hang them in the correct sequence.

Speccing a lobby or feature wall?

Send us the wall dimensions and preferred vein pattern, and we'll recommend a panel set and send pricing.
