FAQ

Common questions from buyers.

What are 3D wall panels made from?

Our 3D wall panels are available in a gypsum, PU or MDF core, each offering a different balance of weight, durability and finish, chosen based on the application and budget.

Do 3D wall panels come pre-painted?

Panels are available either primed and paint-ready, so you can colour-match on site, or in factory-applied finishes for faster turnaround where a standard colour is acceptable.

Can 3D panels be used on ceilings as well as walls?

Yes, the modular 500mm x 500mm tile format is commonly used on feature ceilings as well as accent walls and reception counters, wherever a sculptural, textured surface is wanted.

Planning an accent wall or feature ceiling?

Tell us the surface area and preferred texture, and we'll recommend a panel pattern and send a quote.
