In Detail

Every blind type, up close.

Zebra Blinds

Sheer and solid bands, one chain.

Zebra (day-night) blinds alternate translucent and opaque fabric strips on a single roll, so one chain adjusts light from sheer to near-blackout. Popular for living rooms and offices that need flexible privacy through the day.

Get a Zebra Blinds Quote
Roller Blinds

The standard for offices and homes.

A single fabric panel rolls onto a top tube, in blackout, dim-out or light-filter grades. Clean, low-maintenance and available in printed or plain finishes for conference rooms, bedrooms and full-height glazing.

Get a Roller Blinds Quote
Vertical Blinds

Built for large glazing.

PVC and fabric louvers that stack neatly to one or both sides, rotating for light control without the full stack depth of a roller. The default choice for sliding doors, partitions and boardroom-width windows.

Get a Vertical Blinds Quote
Aluminium Venetian Blinds

Slim slats, precise control.

25mm and 50mm powder-coated aluminium slats tilt independently of height, giving fine-grained light control. Moisture-resistant, so they suit kitchens, bathrooms and office pantries as well as standard windows.

Get an Aluminium Blinds Quote
Wooden Venetian Blinds

A warmer, furniture-grade finish.

Natural or stained basswood slats, generally 50mm, for a heavier, warmer look than aluminium. A common choice for studies, living rooms and hospitality interiors where the blind is part of the decor, not hidden by it.

Get a Wooden Blinds Quote
Custom Printed Blinds

Your design, digitally printed.

Roller and zebra fabrics digitally printed with a pattern, artwork or brand element, matched panel to panel across multiple windows. Popular for feature walls of glazing in retail, hospitality and statement homes.

FAQ

Common questions from buyers.

What is the minimum order quantity for window blinds?

MOQs vary by fabric and mechanism. Sample sets are available for spec approval before you place a bulk order; ask us for the current minimum on your chosen range.

Do you supply motorised blinds?

Yes, motorised roller and vertical blinds are available for office and hospitality projects, alongside standard manual-chain and cordless options.

How long does a bulk blinds order take to dispatch?

Standard fabric orders typically dispatch within 7-10 working days of confirmed measurements; custom prints and motorised units take longer. Sample swatches ship within 48 hours.

Need blinds for a live site?

Send us room dimensions and fabric preference, and we'll return a quote with lead time the same day.
