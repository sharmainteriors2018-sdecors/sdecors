About Us

Sharma Interiors is the firm. SDecors is the name on the box.

We supply the surfaces that make a space look finished: window blinds, wallpaper, wall panels, book-match and UV marble sheets, 3D panels, and wooden or carpet flooring, to architects, designers, contractors, dealers and homeowners, wholesale and retail.

Why Buyers Work With Us

One supplier across ten-plus interior products.

Most projects need more than one surface category: blinds for the glazing, panels for the feature wall, flooring underfoot. Sourcing all of it from one supplier means one point of contact, one invoice cycle, and specs that are checked against each other rather than sourced in isolation.
