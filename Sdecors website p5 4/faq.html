General

Ordering & company

Do you sell to homeowners, or only to the trade?

Both. SDecors is a wholesale and retail supplier, so we quote single-room homeowner orders the same way we quote site-scale trade orders, just at different volumes and pricing tiers.

Can I get samples before I order?

Yes. Physical swatches or sample sets are available across every category before you commit to a full order, so specs get approved once.

Do you deliver across India?

Yes, we dispatch pan-India from our Delhi office. Lead times vary by category and are confirmed at the time of quote.

What is the minimum order quantity?

MOQs vary by product and material. Retail-scale orders for a single room are welcome, and we also support site-scale project quantities. Ask us for the current minimum on your chosen range.

How do I get a quote?

Fill in the enquiry form on our Contact page, WhatsApp us at +91 83681 21845, or call the same number. We typically respond within 48 hours with samples or a formal quote.

Window Blinds

Blinds

Do you supply motorised blinds?

Yes, motorised roller and vertical blinds are available for office and hospitality projects, alongside standard manual-chain and cordless options.

How long does a blinds order take to dispatch?

Standard fabric orders typically dispatch within 7-10 working days of confirmed measurements; custom prints and motorised units take longer. Sample swatches ship within 48 hours.

Wallpaper

Wallpaper

Will every roll match, even for a large wall?

Yes, we supply matched dye-lot batches, so every roll used on a single wall or room reads as one continuous surface.

Can you print a custom design?

Yes, brand-led murals and bespoke artwork can be printed to your specification. Custom print orders take longer to lead time; ask us for the current turnaround.

Wall Panels, Marble & Flooring

Panels & flooring

How is a UV marble sheet different from natural marble?

UV marble sheets give the look and gloss of natural marble at a fraction of the weight, are scratch resistant, and install faster since they don't need on-site polishing or curing time.

Can wooden flooring go over an existing floor?

In most cases yes, engineered and laminate flooring can be laid over a suitably level existing floor with the right underlay; send us the area and current surface and we'll confirm.

Still have a question?

Send us your project details, and we'll come back with a straight answer, not a generic brochure.
