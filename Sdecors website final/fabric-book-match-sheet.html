Specifications

Embossed, sequenced and tactile.

FAQ

Common questions from buyers.

What is a fabric book match sheet?

A fabric book match sheet is a textured, embossed fabric-laminate sheet mounted on an HDHMR or plywood backing, cut and hung in mirrored pairs so the motif — leaf, medallion or geometric — reflects symmetrically across the join.

What motifs are available?

Common motifs include botanical leaf embossing, radial sunburst medallions, and linear geometric fan patterns, each available in a range of neutral and metallic-accented colourways.

Is a fabric book match sheet waterproof?

Yes. Despite the fabric-look texture, the sheet is a waterproof, termite-proof and stain-resistant composite, suitable for both residential and commercial interiors.

Speccing a lobby or feature wall?

Send us the wall dimensions and preferred motif, and we'll recommend a sheet set and send pricing.
