FAQ

Common questions from buyers.

Which pile height should I choose for a balcony or terrace?

25mm grass is the best fit for balconies, patios and rooftop gardens — it gives a neat, freshly-mowed look, is easy to clean and handles high foot traffic with minimal maintenance.

Is artificial grass safe for children and pets?

Yes. 35mm and 50mm grass are commonly used for pet-friendly lawns and children's play areas — both have excellent drainage and the 50mm range adds extra cushioning and impact absorption underfoot.

Can sports turf be customised for different games?

Yes, sports turf pile height is specified by sport, typically from 15mm for tennis and hockey up to 50mm+ for football and rugby, with traction, ball roll and shock absorption engineered for the specific game.

Landscaping a lawn or a full pitch?

Send us the area and use case, and we'll recommend a pile height and send pricing.
