FAQ

Common questions from buyers.

What is a Wooden Vectra Panel made of?

Wooden Vectra Panels are made from HDHMR (High-Density High Moisture Resistance) board, finished with a 3D embossed, fluted or laser-cut geometric pattern in a matte or embossed surface.

Can Wooden Vectra Panels be painted?

Yes. The HDHMR base is paintable, so panels can be site-painted or touched up to match a project's colour scheme after installation.

What sizes and thicknesses are available?

Panels are supplied in 8 ft x 2 ft or 8 ft x 4 ft sheets, in thicknesses from 5.5 mm to 8 mm, with 6 mm and 8 mm the most popular for feature walls.

Speccing a lobby or feature wall?

Send us the wall dimensions and preferred pattern, and we'll recommend a panel set and send pricing.
