FAQ

Common questions from buyers.

What is a marble book match sheet?

A marble book match sheet is a lightweight HPL, PVC or acrylic sheet printed or engineered with a marble vein, cut and hung in mirrored pairs so the pattern reflects symmetrically across the join, like natural bookmatched stone but at a fraction of the weight.

How is it different from a natural stone book-match panel?

A marble book match sheet uses a printed or engineered surface on an HDHMR or plywood backing, so it is far lighter and faster to install than a natural stone veneer panel, while still giving the mirrored, symmetrical bookmatch look.

What sizes and thicknesses are available?

Standard sheets are 8 ft x 4 ft, 1 mm to 5 mm thick depending on the material base, with seamless expanding configurations up to 8 ft x 12 ft or larger for bigger walls.

Speccing a lobby or feature wall?

Send us the wall dimensions and preferred vein pattern, and we'll recommend a sheet set and send pricing.
