FAQ

Common questions from buyers.

What is a book match sheet?

A book match sheet is a symmetrical, mirror-image surface sheet — either marble-pattern or fabric-textured — mounted on an HDHMR or plywood backing, hung so the pattern reflects across the centre join like the pages of an open book.

What's the difference between the Marble and Fabric book match sheet?

The Marble Book Match Sheet uses a printed or engineered marble vein in a glossy or matte finish. The Fabric Book Match Sheet uses a textured, embossed fabric-laminate surface for a softer, tactile finish. Both are supplied in the same sizes and mirrored layout.

What sizes are book match sheets available in?

Standard sheets are 8 ft x 4 ft. Seamless expanding configurations of 8 ft x 8 ft, 8 ft x 12 ft or larger are available for taller or wider feature walls.
